Delicious Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta or guanciale until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Pecorino Romano, Parmesan, minced garlic, and black pepper.
  4. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the pan with the pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper on top.
